The Idea

While out to dinner several times during Summer 2014, I thought a lot about how annoying splitting checks among friends can be. I had just started using Venmo and saw an opportunity to make the process of splitting a check way easier. My friend and roommate at the time Gary and I decided to try our hand at learning Objective-C.

Iterative Design

It needed to be fast, simple and clutter free. Early drawings of Split! introduced me to some of the challenges of mobile -- working with very little screen real estate,

Takeaways

Although we never really put time into building a production-ready version of Split! once school started it was a great design exercise for me that summer as I was just beginning my adventure into the Product Design major at Stanford. I learned a lot about how iterative of a process design is, ----- and most importantly -----. I'm hoping to actually turn this design into a function app in Swift when I take CS193P (iPhone Application Programming) during Winter 2016.
